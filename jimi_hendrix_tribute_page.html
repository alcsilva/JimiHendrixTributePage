Jimi Hendrix

(1942-1970)


The guy who revolutionized the way of playing guitar


Jimi Hendrix on stage at Woodstock in 1969, playing his classical white left-handed Fender-Stratocaster electric guitar.

Here's a time line of Jimi's life and musical career:

  • 1942 - Born in Seattle, USA;
  • 1963 - Moves to New York, where he plays with Little Richard;
  • 1965 - Found his first band, "Jimmy James and The Blues Flames";
  • 1966 - Meets Chas Chandler (The Animals bassist) and moves to London;
  • 1967 - Found two more bands: "Jimi Hendrix Experience" and "Band of Gypsys";
  • 1967 - Release his first album, "Are you experienced?";
  • 1967 - Release his second album, "Axis: Bold as Love";
  • 1968 - The third one, "Electric Ladyland";
  • 1969 - Perfoms at Woodstock festival;
  • 1970 - Dies in London, at age of 27;
